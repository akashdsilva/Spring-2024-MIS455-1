About Samsung

Samsung is a multinational conglomerate known for its extensive range of products and services, ranging from electronics to construction, finance, and more. Here's an overview covering various aspects of Samsung

History

  • Founded in 1938 by Lee Byung-Chul as a trading company in South Korea.
  • Entered the electronics industry in the late 1960s and started producing black-and-white TVs.
  • Rapidly expanded into various sectors, including semiconductors, telecommunications, and home appliances.
  • Became one of the world's largest technology companies, competing with the likes of Apple, Huawei, and Sony.






Innovation and Technology

  • Samsung is known for its commitment to innovation and investing heavily in research and development.
  • It has filed numerous patents and introduced groundbreaking technologies in various fields, including mobile communications, display technology, and semiconductor manufacturing
  • Samsung's Galaxy smartphones, in particular, have been at the forefront of mobile innovation, introducing features like edge-to-edge displays, foldable screens, and advanced camera systems.
